<%= heading2 %>{Figures with tikz}

The \href{https://ctan.org/pkg/pgf}{tikz} package creates graphics programmatically.
With this package, grids or other regular structures can be generated easily.

<%- bexample %>
\begin{figure}[ht]
  \centering
  \begin{tikzpicture}
    \draw(0,0) rectangle (4,4);
    \foreach \x in {0.5,1,1.5,2,2.5,3,3.5}
    \foreach \y in {0.5,1,1.5,2,2.5,3,3.5}
    \draw(\x,\y) circle (1pt);
  \end{tikzpicture}
  \caption{A regular grid generated easily with two for loops.}\label{fig:tikz_example}
\end{figure}
<%- eexample %>
